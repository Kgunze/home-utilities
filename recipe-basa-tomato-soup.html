Recipes / Broth

Basa tomato soup for light weeknight dinners.

A quick broth recipe that uses planner basa fillet, tomatoes, herbs, and a clean sour-savory balance.

Read the methodBack to recipe shelf

Story

Bright, clean, and planner-friendly.

This is the sort of soup that makes use of fish and tomatoes without becoming heavy.

Basa is mild, so tomato, scallion, dill, and a little fish sauce do the work of carrying the bowl.

It fits well on weeks when the planner already includes fish fillet, cucumber, tomatoes, and fresh greens on the side.

Method

Keep the fish delicate.

  1. Saute tomato, shallot, and a little garlic until juicy.
  2. Add light broth or water, then simmer briefly before sliding in basa fillet pieces.
  3. Finish with herbs, fish sauce, and black pepper once the fish is just cooked.

Planner ingredients

  • Basa fillet
  • Tomatoes
  • Shallot or garlic
  • Fresh herbs
