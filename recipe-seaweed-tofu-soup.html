Recipes / Broth

Seaweed tofu soup for the easiest planner night.

This is the fast soup built directly from tofu and seaweed already listed in the shopping planner.

Read the methodBack to recipe shelf

Story

A calm bowl for reset days.

When the planner gives you tofu and seaweed, this is one of the quickest ways to make them feel complete.

The broth stays light, letting the tofu stay soft and the seaweed do most of the work.

It fits well beside rice, grilled protein, or as a quiet second dish on a heavier dinner table.

Method

Short simmer, soft finish.

  1. Bring light stock or water to a simmer with garlic or ginger if desired.
  2. Add seaweed and tofu cubes, then season with salt, soy, or fish sauce lightly.
  3. Finish with scallion and serve immediately.

Planner ingredients

  • Tofu
  • Seaweed
  • Scallion
  • Light stock
