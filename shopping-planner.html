Translated introduction

Weekly shopping list based on the approved 30-day menu.

Repeated ingredients have been combined within each week so a parent can shop only once or twice weekly. Quantities are intended for 3 people over 7 days, at approximately 150k per day, not including rice and seasonings.

Average weekly purchase

A fixed formula you can reuse every week.

  • Pork: about 900g
  • Mixed seafood: about 700-900g
  • Beef: about 300g
  • Spare ribs: about 500g
  • Chicken: about half a bird
  • Eggs: 10-12
  • Vegetables: 4-5kg
  • Tofu: 4 blocks

This formula can be reused for any week without needing to recalculate the whole menu.

Smart shopping tips

Simple ways to save effort and cost.

  • Buy meat once, portion it into bags, then freeze it.
  • Buy vegetables twice per week to keep them fresher and cheaper.
  • Buy frozen seafood to save about 25-35%.
