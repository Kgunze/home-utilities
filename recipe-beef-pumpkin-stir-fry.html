Recipes / Non-broth

Beef and pumpkin stir-fry for fast planner cooking.

A skillet recipe that uses planner beef and pumpkin without needing a long prep window.

Read the methodBack to recipe shelf

Story

Fast heat, soft sweetness, quick dinner.

Beef cooks fast, pumpkin takes flavor well, and the whole dish works when the evening needs to stay efficient.

Thin-sliced beef and small pumpkin pieces let the pan stay quick rather than complicated.

It pairs well with rice and whatever greens from the planner are already waiting in the fridge.

Method

Use a hot pan and keep the beef moving.

  1. Cook pumpkin first until almost tender.
  2. Add garlic, then beef, and stir-fry quickly over high heat.
  3. Season with fish sauce, black pepper, and a little sugar if needed.

Planner ingredients

  • Beef
  • Pumpkin
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
