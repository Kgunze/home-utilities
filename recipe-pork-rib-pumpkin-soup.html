Recipes / Broth

Pork rib pumpkin soup for sweet, gentle comfort.

This broth turns planner ribs and pumpkin into a home-style soup that feels soft and filling without being heavy.

Read the methodBack to recipe shelf

Story

Sweet broth, patient simmer.

Ribs bring the savory base. Pumpkin turns the broth mellow and family-friendly.

This is a soup that feels especially right when the week has a lot of rice meals and needs one softer bowl in between.

The ingredients come straight from the planner, which makes it a reliable option rather than a special-event dish.

Method

Simmer until the pumpkin yields.

  1. Parboil or rinse ribs, then simmer until the broth tastes sweet and meaty.
  2. Add pumpkin pieces and cook until tender but not falling apart.
  3. Season simply and finish with pepper or scallion.

Planner ingredients

  • Spare ribs
  • Pumpkin
  • Scallion
  • Pepper
